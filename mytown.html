About Edinburgh

Edinburgh is the capital of Scotland. The population is around 500,000. Edinburgh has famous landmarks such as, Edinburgh Castle, Arthur's Seat, Holyrood Palace and more. The city also has some famous streets such as, Princes Street (for shopping), the Royal Mile (most famous in Edinburgh), Victoria Street (prettiest street), George Street (for nightlife) and more. Edinburgh gets around 4 million tourists a year with most coming in August for Edinburgh Festival which is 500,000 tourists. Edinburgh is known for its rich history such as home of the encyclopedia, grave robbers, Greyfriars Bobby, Burke and Hare and more
